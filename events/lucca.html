About the Workshop

Join us for our annual Winter Research Workshop, a three-day event designed to bring together PhD students and postdoctoral researchers from various disciplines. This workshop provides an excellent opportunity to present your research, learn from peers, and build lasting professional connections.

The workshop will feature two keynote presentations from more senior researchers, presentation sessions, and dedicated networking sessions. Whether you're looking to get feedback on your work, explore new research directions, or simply connect with fellow early-career researchers, this event offers something for everyone.

Venue Information

The workshop will be held at the Sagrestia, in San Francesco building. The venue is easily accessible by public transportation and offers modern conference facilities with audio-visual equipment.

Address: Sagrestia IMT, Piazza San Francesco 17, Lucca
Parking: Available on campus (limited spots)
Public Transport: Bus lines 12, 34, 56 to "University Stop"
